Game Instructions

Enemy Types

Standard Raven

Green color. Average speed and health. Worth 1 point.

Speedy Raven

Yellow color. Fast but fragile. Moves in a zigzag pattern. Worth 2 points.

Tank Raven

Pink color. Slow but tough. Occasionally charges forward. Worth 3 points.

Power-Ups

Extra Life

Pink heart. Gives you an additional life.

Clear Screen

Yellow star. Destroys all ravens on screen and awards points.

Slow Time

Cyan clock. Slows down all ravens for 5 seconds.

How to Play

Click or tap on ravens to shoot them down. Build combos by hitting ravens in succession for bonus points. Don't let ravens reach the left side of the screen or you'll lose a life!
